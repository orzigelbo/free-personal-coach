COACHG Coach will help you keep fit while the gyms are closed. We will prepare a professional fitness program for you that will personally suit your time and equipment. And all, for free!.

Get stared

Why are we doing this for free?

During the COVID-19 a lot of people lost the ability to keep their regular workout routine as a result of the closed gyms and the prolonged stay in homes. We decided to give back to the community and and allow everyone to get the best information in order to keep fit at home.

Do I need equipment?

Most of our programs do not require equipment, if you do have equipment such as weights, rubber bands or a pullup bar, you can get a program that will help you use them properly.
